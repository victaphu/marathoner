CANDIDACY MEMBERSHIP

The Candidacy membership is a unique digital membership offered to patrons of the Sydney Marathon to support the events qualification into the Abbotts World Marathon Majors classification. The membership is open to all entrants to the 2023 Sydney Marathon event and provides the holder with privileges and benefits to future Sydney Marathon events and VIP deals with sponsors:

  • Limited edition of 1000 memberships
  • Provides the holder with guaranteed entry to the Sydney Marathon for a period of 10 years
  • Locked-in early bird entrance fee for all holders
  • Exclusive in-store discounts with ASICS – 10% off of all footwear and 15% of apparel
  • Tickets to Athletics Australia events and track days
  • Exclusive access to Athletics Australia elite athletes Q&A and ask me anything online sessions
  • Training and Masterclass content produced by Athletics Australia
  • Priced at $100 per memberships
  • Memberships are transferable
